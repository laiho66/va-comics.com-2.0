Cookie Policy

This website uses a minimal number of cookie files to ensure optimal navigation and the correct functioning of the user interface.

1. What are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. It helps the platform remember certain navigation preferences over a period of time to deliver a fluid experience.

2. What Types of Cookies Do We Use?

We use exclusively Technical (Essential) Cookies. These are strictly required for the performance of the interface, such as maintaining the active states of the collapsible menu sidebar or ensuring the seamless loading of our blurred background overlays. This website DOES NOT utilize any marketing, tracking, advertising, or behavioral profiling cookies.

3. Managing and Deleting Cookies

You can manage, block, or delete cookies at any time directly through your browser settings (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, etc.). Please note that disabling technical cookies may cause minor visual performance drops or layout bugs within the VA Comics terminal interface.
